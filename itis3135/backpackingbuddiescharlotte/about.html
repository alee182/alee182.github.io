About Us

Welcome to the Charlotte Backpacking Club, a vibrant and welcoming community of outdoor enthusiasts based in the heart of Charlotte, North Carolina, who are united by a shared passion for backpacking, hiking, and experiencing the wonders of the natural world firsthand.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a strong and supportive network for individuals who love spending time in the outdoors, while also encouraging responsible stewardship of the environment through conservation efforts, educational outreach, and the promotion of sustainable practices that minimize our impact on the trails and wild places we cherish.

What We Do

We organize a wide variety of activities, including scenic day hikes on local trails, challenging multi-day backpacking trips through some of the most beautiful landscapes in the region, skill-building workshops focused on outdoor preparedness, and community events that emphasize the importance of Leave No Trace principles and active participation in conservation initiatives.

Join Us!

No matter if you are a seasoned thru-hiker with thousands of miles under your boots or a beginner taking your very first steps on the trail, the Charlotte Backpacking Club offers a welcoming space for everyone to grow their skills, build lifelong friendships, and contribute to a cause that protects our natural spaces for generations to come; we would love for you to be part of our journey.
